# 🌍 EcoSphere AI
### AI-Powered ESG Management & Employee Sustainability Platform

> **Empowering organizations to measure, improve, and gamify sustainability through AI-driven ESG intelligence.**

---

## 📖 Overview

EcoSphere AI is a modern ESG (Environmental, Social & Governance) Management Platform designed to help organizations track sustainability initiatives, encourage employee participation, and generate actionable ESG insights using Artificial Intelligence.

The platform combines **ESG Management**, **Gamification**, and **AI** into one intelligent ecosystem where every employee action contributes to the organization's sustainability goals.

---

## 🚀 Problem Statement

Organizations struggle to measure and improve ESG performance due to disconnected systems, manual reporting, and low employee engagement.

EcoSphere AI solves this by providing:

- 🌱 Environmental Tracking
- 🤝 Social Engagement
- 🏛 Governance Monitoring
- 🎮 Gamification
- 🤖 AI-Powered ESG Intelligence
